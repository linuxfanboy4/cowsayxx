Cowsay++

The Ultimate Enhanced Cowsay with More Features

Features

More Animal Support

Enjoy a wider range of animals for your ASCII output.

Text-to-Speech

Support for converting text output to speech.

Speech Bubbles

Get ASCII output with speech bubbles for a more interactive experience.

Custom Animals

Import your own custom animals via a simple file (custom.txt).

Rainbow Rendering

Make your ASCII art pop with rainbow colors!

Convert to PNG

Convert your ASCII output to a PNG image for easy sharing.

Simple Animations

Bring your ASCII art to life with basic frame animations.

Installation

To install Cowsay++, open your terminal and run:

curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/linuxfanboy4/cowsaypp/refs/heads/main/install.sh | bash
